Health care disclaimer


The features included in MDS Manager are intended to help individuals living with MDS to actively participate in their own care by providing a variety of tools for users to track information related to their condition. These tools include: Medical appointments, health care team contacts, medications, test results, indicated physical conditions, and health profile information, and contacts (“Features”). These features are intended to be used by the individual and their caregivers to help manage the patients care.


All health and health related information contained within MDS manager (“ Content”) is intended to be general and informational in nature. Nothing in the Features or Content should be considered for use as a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We advise users to seek the guidance of a physician or other qualified health provider with any questions he/she may have regarding his/her condition or treatment. Although every effort is made to ensure that material within MDS Manager is accurate and timely, it is provided for the convenience of the user and should not be considered official. None of the treatments, products or services described in the content represents or warrants that any particular treatments, products, or services are safe, appropriate, and/or effective for you.


If you have, or suspect that you have, a medical emergency, call your qualified health care professional immediately or dial 911 (in the United States) or an emergency helpline using the nearest telephone.
